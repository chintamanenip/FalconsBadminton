📋 Rules (click to expand)
  • Principle: Equal playing time, varied partners, fluent mix of sexes and skill levels.
  • Peg colours: Ladies = Blue, Men = Tan, Guests = Purple.
  • Picking: First non-guest in queue picks. Guests cannot pick.
  • Ladies priority: If a lady is in top 5, she may pick first for ladies 4 or mixed.
  • After game: Winners return to queue first, losers follow.
  • Break: Remove peg to storage; return it to back of queue when ready.
